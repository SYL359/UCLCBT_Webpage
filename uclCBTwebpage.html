WHY BLOCKCHAIN?

Blockchain technology enables the idea of decentralisation and disruption of the business and finance industry. The idea of ledgers being at the hearth of business and finance is now a questionable one, as for the first time blockchain technologies enable the collaborative creation of digital distributed ledgers with properties and capabilities that go far beyond traditional paper-based ledgers
